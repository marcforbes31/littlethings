Day Three, 16th February 2023

Here we are. A day of ups and downs but ultimately, was still a great one!
Day : 3
Event : The Fight

A rather eventful day.

Chapter 1 : Dim Sum Deja Vu


So we once again kicked things off with Dim Sum. Yes, again. Because you couldn't help yourself can you, Tamin? One meal the day before wasn't enough apparently. Being the very good boyfriend I am, of course I said "Yeah sure why not?". The story from here is similar to the Suria chapter from Day 2, so no need to rinse and repeat. As expected, the food was delicious and fulfilling. It was a very good start to the day, we were going strong and it looked like it was on course to be that way throughout.

Chapter 2 : The Fight


But oh well, it couldn't be that easy now could it? In every movie there has to be a drop point, a CONFLICT, and this was ours. It all started when I wanted to go to Tinyrobot. Well we both know what happened then, right? After Suria, we finally went to Imago but the only problem was, we brought the fight with us as well. We spent so much time not talking to each other and stayed very distant from one another, which I do fully regret. I didn't take any pictures during our fight but I still remember how mad you were. Let me find something to represent you when you were angry on that day hehe.

I think I got that spot on. But in all seriousness, I really hate it when we fight. But fights are normal, we just need to find and commit to an effective way of solving them. Fortunately for the both of us, around half past nine at night, we managed to finally forgive each other. It was a beautiful moment, and it was very heartwarming, I must say. We hugged it out and became the best of friends again yay! "Oh, now that we're back on each other's good side, that means we still have time left to catch our movie!". Yeah that's right!

That's us making up, by the way. And I know you love We Bare Bears.

I mean I guess RM20 for medium popcorn and soda is alright but the rates they charged for that corn and the sausages were just insane. And the worst part is, they weren't even anywhere close to being decent tasting. But hey, food is food so here's some shots of us!

Chapter 4 : FC Barcelona 2-2 Manchester United


I said Ant-Man was mediocre entertainment, but do you know what was NOT mediocre entertainment? Manchester United. They were on fire, even to this day actually. I found it quite cute that you stayed up and watched the game with me for a little bit. Mid-way through the game I decided to cook up those Maggi Hot Cups that we bought the day before, and I knew you were looking forward to that more than the football game. I opened and cooked one Maggi Kari Hot Cup and you practically ate it all. So I cooked the other one and you tried to eat all of that too, but luckily you hated the texture of the noodles, hehe but actually maybe you should have eaten them because them noodles made my throat even worse. A few minutes later you were sound asleep, as I watched the game. The sight of you sleeping comfortably just makes me feel at ease, because you're not awake to bother me. IM KIDDING, IM KIDDING. But in all seriousness, it just makes me feel warm, like you're safe here with me and I will look after you.
